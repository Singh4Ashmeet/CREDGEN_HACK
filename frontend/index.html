What CREDGEN AI Does

Conversational Loan Applications

Guides users through loan applications with natural conversations, asking for necessary information and documents.

Document Processing

Accepts and analyzes uploaded documents (ID, bank statements, income proofs) to extract relevant information.

Fraud Detection

Real-time fraud detection algorithms flag suspicious applications to protect against fraudulent attempts.

Instant Underwriting

Automated risk assessment and credit scoring for immediate preliminary loan decisions.

Loan Offers

Generates personalized loan offers with terms, interest rates, and repayment plans based on risk profile.

Document Generation

Automatically creates sanction letters and loan agreements once terms are accepted.

How to Integrate

Add our AI loan assistant to your website in 2 minutes with a single line of code.

Copy the Embed Code

Add this code before the closing </body> tag of your website.

<!-- Embed CredGen AI Bot on your website -->
<script src="https://credgen.com/widget-ui.js"></script>
<script src="https://credgen.com/chat.js"></script>

That's It

The chat widget will automatically appear on your site. Customize position and colors if needed.
